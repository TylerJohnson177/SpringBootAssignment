Building Techniques

Once you have a building site in mind, the third step is to start building. Based on personal experience, the best building techniques include:

  • Snowball based Technique
  • This technique involves creating or finding large snowballs and stacking them up to create a wall.
  • Digging Technique
  • In order to use this technique, you will need to build in a snowbank, or there should be at least 2 to 3 feet of snow on the ground. This can be accomplished by digging out the snow in the middle, and packing it down on the outside of the fort, making a wall.
  • Snow Block Mold Technique
  • Based on personal experience, this is by far the most efficient way to build a fort, it uses the least amount of snow, and looks the coolest. You can use tupperware containers, buckets, or large rubbermaid bins if you wish. What I find to work the best is the snow block molds you can get at your local supermarket. Simply pack the snow into the mold, flip it over and a snow block should come out. Stack the blocks in a staggered pattern for the best strength.
