Finding a Good Building Site

The second step to building a snowfort is finding a place to build, Based on personal experience, the best snowfort building sites include:

  • Backyards
  • This is the most common place for snowfort building. Advantages of building in the backyard include, you do not have to carry your equipment very far, and you can see it from your window. Disadvantages include, not as much snow to work with(You will be surprised how much snow you use), and the ground may not be completely flat.
  • In Snowbanks
  • Snowbanks is another common place for snowfort building. Advantages include, it will look very cool, and you have lots of snow to work with. Disadvantages include a very steep slope, and snowbanks are usually located near a road or parking lot.
  • Frozen Lakes
  • Based on experience, frozen lakes is by far the best building site for snowforts. Advantages include, very open area for expansion, lots of snow to work with, and a completely flat surface. Disadvatages include, high winds, drifting snow, and frozen lakes can be dangerous if the ice is not tested properly, make sure there is at least 6-8 inches of ice when the temperature is above freezing.
