Ideal Building Conditions

The first step to building a snowfort is to check the weather report, the amount of snow on the ground, and the upcoming weather.

  • Temperature
  • The ideal temperature for snowfort building is between 33 and 37 degrees fahrenheit, any warmer, your fort will melt, any colder, the snow will not pack as well.
  • Snow Amount
  • There should be at least 6 inches of snow on the ground, but the more snow, the better.
  • Temperature in the Coming Days
  • One thing to keep an eye on when building a snowfort, is the temperature in the coming days, if you see it's going to get in the 40s, you may be better off holding back until it gets colder. Ideally, you want a cold spell to occur after completion of the fort to allow the snow to freeze.
  • Upcoming Weather Events
  • When building snowforts, avoid building during or right before major winter storms, you don't want all your hard work to get buried! Not only that, you will have more snow to work with after the storm.
